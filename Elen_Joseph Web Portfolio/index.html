Elen Joseph

Business Administrator graduated in 2011 from the University of Guarulhos - Sao Paulo Brazil, former Brazilian Portuguese Teacher to Canadians Diplomats at École Diplomatique Bisson (ministère Des Affaires Étrangères)- Gatineau Canada and Motivational Speaker. Interactive Media Creator graduating from Algonquin College in April 2020. Married to Canadian Economics Professor Troy Joseph, both lovers of Knowledge, Travel, and Culture. Elen had been running an in-company English School called VIP Class when still in Brazil 2011-2014 and delivering motivational lectures in Public Colleges such as ETEC and philanthropic institutions since 2009. She arrived in Canada on August 22, since then she has been working as an Event Management planer for companies such as Centurion Conference Center, Best Western Hotel, Albert At Bay Hotel, Parliament Hill, National Bank of Canada, Art Gallery, Museums, City Hall and Many others places. She has also worked as a Brazilian Portuguese teacher to Canadian Diplomats and as an Exchange Program consultant to foreign students to Teacher, Canada Ponto, 4U-Study Exchange programs Agencies .
